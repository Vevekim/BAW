Tuesday, March 16th 2021

This seminar will educate attendees on H.R. 1109, which aims to provide students mental health services from a bill to a law. As a bipartisan mental health policy, I feel that H.R. 1109 is an excellent cause for college students' support. I will explain how to run a workshop that educates college students on a bill that addresses an issue like H.R. 1109 and conveys to lawmakers why they should co-sponsor a bill so it becomes a law. By increasing policy advocacy in college students it conveys the message that we can change the world and politics doesn't stop on election day. This seminar will serve as a call to action to get attendees involved in issue advocacy year-round.
